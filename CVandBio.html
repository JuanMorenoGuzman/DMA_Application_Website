BIO

Juan Moreno is a Digital Artist that has been inspired all throughout his life by the artistic world of videogames, as well as the fast evolving and fascinating technology, due to this he now makes works that reflect a futuristic environments or fantasy inspired ones. He currently attends San Jose State University, where he has enhanced his understanding of design and his skills on a wide range of digital media software, which have allowed him to create dynamic and unique designs. Juan hopes to become a great designer where he can help the world look a bit more futuristic or work with video games and help to contribute to the creation of a fictional, yet artistic world.
