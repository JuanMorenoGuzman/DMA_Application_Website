STATEMENT OF PURPOSE

My name is Juan Moreno Guzman, and I am currently studying Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. As an artist I am really interested in technology, video game art and fictional heroes. These have inspired me to become an artist and I always strive to create and design something dynamic, elegant, and powerful. I respect, appreciate, and get inspired by all forms of art that make up a video game (writing, music, art, design, code, etc.) and not many do. The Digital world also inspires me because I can see how humanity continues to evolve through the use of technology and at the same time is able to make it into an artform.

For my career goals I hope to continue to hone my skills in the creation of artwork through the use of Digital Media and also to improve on creating more dynamic, elegant and powerful designs that can help me achieve a dream of working with Digital Art and new Media that will allow me to create and give life to video games or help the world look more vibrant and futuristic.
